Green Light Group Description

Our group is composed of four students who work together to create various media that focuses on educating and informing audiences on environmental sustainability for a Virginia Tech Writing and Digital Media course. Members include freshman, Melissa Nazario; sophomore, Lauren Holt; junior, Dana Kelly and senior, Matt Hochkammer.

Published Media

Newsletter

By using TinyLetter, a personal newsletter service, Green Light has published 10 newsletters and circulated them to their English 3844 peers via email. These newsletters discuss an array of environmetalist topics, spanning from climate change to Apple's clean-energy practices. Click on the icon above to read "Talking Trash," a short article that provides a few helpful tips on how to improve your recycling habits!

Podcast

Curious about GMO's? Green Light is too! In fact, we have produced a Podcast discussing GMOs in detail. In under 15 minutes, Green Light explores what GMOs are, the nature of how they work, and the implications they have for environmetal practices of farming, as well as their potential health effects. Are you a Virginia Tech student looking to get involved in an evironmetal group on campus? Look no further! Students for Sustainable Practice is a group of Hokies that act as a liaison between our student body and the university administration.
